2024 Honda Accord (Base and Touring trims)
The 2024 Honda Accord continues to be a well-balanced midsize sedan offering efficient powertrains, comfortable interior packaging, and modern safety features. Engine options include a 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 producing 192 hp and 192 lb-ft of torque, and an available 2.0-liter turbo inline-4 on higher trims making 252 hp and 273 lb-ft. 2024 Toyota Camry (LE / XSE)
The 2024 Toyota Camry remains a staple in the midsize segment, offered with a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated inline-4 with 203 hp and 184 lb-ft, a more powerful 3.5-liter V6 with 301 hp and 267 lb-ft for sportier trims, and a hybrid option pairing the 2.5 with electric motors for combined system output in the range of 208–215 hp. Transmissions are an 8-speed automatic for higher-output engines and an electronically-controlled continuously variable transmission (eCVT) for hybrid models. Fuel economy: base 2.5L achieves about 28 city / 39 highway mpg; hybrid variants typically return 51 combined mpg. Dimensions: wheelbase 111.2 in, length 192.1 in, width 72.4 in; curb weight ranges ~3,200–3,600 lb. Safety package includes Toyota Safety Sense with pre-collision system (with pedestrian detection), lane departure alert with steering assist, and dynamic radar cruise control.
2024 Tesla Model 3 (Standard Range Plus / Long Range) 
The 2024 Tesla Model 3 is a compact electric sedan available in rear-wheel-drive Standard Range and dual-motor all-wheel-drive Long Range and Performance variants. Battery capacities vary between about 55–82 kWh usable depending on the variant and software-defined limits. Performance: Standard Range is rated at approximately 0–60 mph in 5.3 sec and an EPA range around 260 miles; Long Range AWD reduces 0–60 to ~4.2 sec with an EPA range near 320 miles; Performance cuts 0–60 to around 3.1 sec with a lower range depending on wheel choice. Charging: with Tesla Supercharger V3, peak power up to ~250 kW on compatible sites; typical 10–80% DC fast charging times vary from 20 to 40 minutes depending on conditions. Key specs include a near-instant torque delivery, single-pedal driving mode, regenerative braking levels, and software-enabled driver-assist features (Autopilot with optional Full Self-Driving). Interior notes: minimalist cabin, 15-inch central display, over-the-air updates, and a flat, nearly featureless center console. Safety highlights include high crash-test ratings in many regions and a strong battery pack structural design."""
